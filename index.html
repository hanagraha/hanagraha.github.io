Notable Courses

Notable courses taken during the Masters in Geo-Information Sciences and Bachelors in International Land and Water Management. I have studied in Wageningen University and Research and University of California, Berkeley.

Geoscripting

This course introduced me to coding both in Python and R for spatial analysis. We practiced data acquisition to manipulation to analysis and visualization. In the final few weeks, I was involved in a project for Space4Good to improve deforestation detection in Kalimantan, Indonesia.

Machine Learning

In this course, we covered machine learning approaches for both regression and classification. With an equally strong emphasis on theory and practice, we learned to navigate different algorithms and statistical tools.

Advanced Earth Observation

In this course we built upon the general course Remote Sensing. We studied RADAR and LiDAR techniques in further detail and explored different remote sensing software such as CloudCompare, Agisoft Metashape, Google Earth Engine, and R. In the final two weeks of the course, I wrote a methodology paper on interpreting forest vulnerabilit using MODIS versus VIIRS NDVI time series.
